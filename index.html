How it works

1
Upload your Google Forms CSV — names, matric numbers, and responses are read automatically.
2
Integrity check — AI writing patterns and duplicate submissions are flagged.
3
Auto marking runs — a built-in marking guide checks each response for required concepts, keywords, word count, and citation patterns. Scores are suggested automatically.
4
You review and override — every score is editable. Quick buttons (0 / half / full) speed up reviewing. You have the final say.
5
Download results — CSV with names, scores, grades, flags, and part-by-part marks.
