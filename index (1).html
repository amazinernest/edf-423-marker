How it works

1
Upload your Google Forms CSV — the tool reads names, matric numbers, and responses automatically.
2
Integrity check — the tool scans all responses for AI writing patterns and flags any students whose work looks copied from each other.
3
Mark each student using the rubric for their question. Flags appear on the marking card.
4
Download results as CSV — includes scores, grades, AI flag, and duplicate flag for every student.
